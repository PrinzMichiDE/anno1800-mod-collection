Old-Town v1.3:

!!! This mod may not be compatible with the "Construction Menu Cleanup" mod !!!

This mod adds some buildings from Anno 1701 and 1404. Engineers and investors have a new building need, which can be satisfied by the added buildings.

Engineers have the need for higher culture, which is satisfied by the opera.

For investors, the need for power comes, which is satisfied by the Senate.

Oldtown-townhouse:

- unlocked at 600 Artisians

Old-town mansions:

- unlocked at 1500 engineers

Old-town villas:

- unlocked at 2500 investors

Opera:

- Unlocked at 20 old-town mansions

Senate:

- unlocked at 20 old town villas

Imperial Cathedral (Monument):

- satisfies the need of the church, higher culture and power

- All buildings on the island get +5 satisfaction, -5% consumption of all goods, -20% illness, -20% insurrection

- First 3 stages unlocked at 7500 investors, last stage unlocked at 12500 investors


In addition, a new church version is buildable (Shift + v), a small hospital (doctors building; unlocked at 600 artisians), an inn is buildable (unlocked at 1500 investors) a new town hall is buildable (unlocked at 2500 investors) and a triumphal arch can be built, through which one  a road connection can be established (Shift + v for right and left) (unlocked at 2500 investors).

The additional town hall has a higher radius and one more slot (scroll in the information window of the town hall)


!!! This mod is compatible with previously started savegames. No unlocker is needed !!!


The building-menus can be found at engineer- & investor-building-menu
