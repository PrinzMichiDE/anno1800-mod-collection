This mod unlocks the exclusive ships you either needed an extremely rare drop/reward to unlock... or had to purchase from pirates. This allows you to build a shipyard and build the exclusive ships as long as you have an Artisan on your island. No deviation from the building requirements however for each ship.

This mod unlocks:
(Shipyard)
Pirate Gunboat
Pirate Frigate
Pirate Ship-of-the-Line
Royal Ship-of-the-Line
(Steam Shipyard)
Pirate Monitor
Pyrothian Monitor
Pyrothian Warship
Pyrothian Battle Cruiser
Extravaganza Steamer
