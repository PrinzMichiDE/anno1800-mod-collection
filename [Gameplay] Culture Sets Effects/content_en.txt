This mod replaces some useless culture sets effects with more useful effects.

Aegean Cultures, buff the World's Fair, attractiveness +1000
Bronze Age, buff all mines and quarries, productivity +50%
Icebound, buff all food and drink production buildings, productivity +50%
Luminaries, buff all shipyards,construction cost -10%, construction time -50%
Rainforest, buff all cabins and huts, productivity +50%, forest density -10%

note, you may need to rebuild or rellocate buildings to let it take effect
